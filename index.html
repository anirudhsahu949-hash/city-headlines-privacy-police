1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how City Headlines ("we", "us", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application. City Headlines is a local news app that lets you read news stories, create an account, and share articles. By using the app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information needed to run the app:

Information When it is collected Why
Email address When you sign up or log in To create and secure your account
Password When you sign up or log in To authenticate you. Passwords are handled and stored securely by Firebase Authentication; we never see or store them in plain text.
Account profile (email, account role, account creation date) Automatically when your account is created To manage your account and determine which features you can access

We do not collect your precise or approximate location, your contacts, your photos, or any device identifiers for advertising. The app does not show ads and does not sell your data.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create, secure, and manage your account.
  • To let you log in and stay signed in.
  • To provide app features such as reading and sharing news.
  • To maintain the security and proper functioning of the app.

4. Third-Party Services

City Headlines uses Google Firebase (Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore) to handle sign-in and to store account information and news content. When you use the app, Firebase may process your data on our behalf. Firebase is operated by Google, and its handling of data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy.

When you share an article, the app opens your device's native share menu. Where you send that content (messaging apps, email, social media, etc.) is your choice, and any data you share that way is handled by those services under their own privacy policies.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We only share data with the service providers (Firebase / Google) that are necessary to operate the app, or where we are legally required to do so (for example, in response to a valid legal request).

6. Data Retention

We keep your account information for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we remove your associated personal information from our active records, except where we are required to keep it for legal or security reasons.

7. Account & Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and associated personal data at any time. To do so, email us at playonsport03@gamil.com from the email address linked to your account, with the subject "Delete my account". We will process your request within a reasonable period.

Google Play requires apps with accounts to offer a way to request data deletion. If you later add an in-app "Delete account" button, mention it here too.

8. Security

We rely on Google Firebase's industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encrypted authentication. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

City Headlines is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your country), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

10. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information, or to object to certain processing. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.
