Account & Data Deletion

City Headlines

Effective date: 05/06/2026

App: City Headlines

Overview

City Headlines lets you create an account to access certain features. You can ask us to delete your account and the personal data linked to it at any time. This page explains how to make that request and what happens to your data.

How to Request Deletion

To delete your account and associated data, send us an email:

  1. Open your email app and write to playonsport03@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject line: Delete my account.
  3. Send the request from the same email address you used to sign in, so we can verify it is really you.

Once we confirm the request belongs to your account, we will process the deletion. We aim to complete it within 30 days of receiving a verified request.

What Data Is Deleted

When your account is deleted, we remove the personal information tied to your account, including:

Data What happens
Your account login (email and password) Deleted from Firebase Authentication
Your account profile (email, account role, account creation date) Deleted from our database (Cloud Firestore)

What May Be Kept

News articles and other public content shown in the app are not personal to you and are not affected by deleting your account. We may also retain a limited amount of information for a short period where we are required to do so by law, or to prevent fraud and abuse. Any such data is kept only as long as necessary and is then permanently removed.

Important to Know

  • Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
  • After deletion, you will not be able to log in with that account again.
  • You are welcome to create a new account at any time in the future.
